Wenia — Dual-Platform Crypto Wallet

Architecting DesignOps governance for a native crypto-wallet, enabling parallel Android & iOS development while defining critical financial flows.

Role Lead Product Designer
Duration 18 months
Year 2021-2022
Team 3 Designers, 8 Engineers, 1 Product Manager

Impact

2 Platforms (iOS & Android)
18mo MVP to multi-product

Overview

Parallel native development. Zero design drift. Critical financial flows standardized.

Wenia is a DeFi platform offering a native crypto-wallet for buying, selling, and managing digital assets. When I joined, the product was building simultaneously for Android and iOS with separate engineering teams—a recipe for design fragmentation in a space where trust depends on visual consistency.

Over 18 months, I established the DesignOps foundation that enabled parallel development without drift. This meant creating platform-aware components, defining critical financial flows (COPW—Crypto Onboarding, Purchase, Withdrawal), and bridging the communication gap between Design and Mobile Engineering.
